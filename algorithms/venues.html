Venues


    Journals

Journal of the ACM (JACM):
    The flagship journal of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), covering all aspects of computer science, including algorithms and data structures.

SIAM Journal on Computing (SICOMP):
    Published by the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM), it covers theoretical computer science, including algorithms, computational complexity, and data structures.

Algorithmica:
    Focuses on algorithm design, analysis, and implementation, as well as related aspects of computer science.

ACM Transactions on Algorithms (TALG):
    Publishes original research of the highest quality dealing with all aspects of algorithms.

    Conferences

Annual ACM Symposium on Theory of Computing (STOC):
    One of the most prestigious conferences in theoretical computer science, covering a wide range of topics including algorithms and data structures.

IEEE Symposium on Foundations of Computer Science (FOCS):
    Along with STOC, it is one of the top conferences in theoretical computer science.

Annual ACM-SIAM Symposium on Discrete Algorithms (SODA):
    Focuses specifically on research in discrete algorithms and data structures.

European Symposium on Algorithms (ESA):
    Covers research in the design and analysis of algorithms, experimental evaluation, and real-world applications.

Symposium on Experimental Algorithms (SEA):
    Focuses on the experimental evaluation of algorithms and data structures.
